CC Global Summit 2015
Logo Contest Open for Voting!

CC Global Summit 2015 is held in Seoul, South Korea, in October 2015, to host people who believe in the values of sharing and openness and create positive change in the world.

The Logo Contest for the CC Global Summit 2015 ran from June 8 through July 7 and submission is now closed.

From established professional designers to students and amateurs, anyone with creative talent are welcome to join!

 

Please help us pick the best logo design for the CC Global Summit 2015!
(You can vote on more than one design; your vote on a certain design counts only once.)

After the online public voting and judging by the CC Selection Committee,
the winning logo will be announced on July 23.
