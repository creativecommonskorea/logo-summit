Logo Contest
Winner Announced!

Thanks to everyone who entered our Logo Contest which ran from June 8 to July 7, 2015.
After online public voting and Selection Committee's judging process, we are now very excited to announce the winner as below.

Congratulations to nareshagrawalart for winning the contest!
The organizers will contact the winner via e-mail regarding the prize.

Thank you all who participated in the CC Global Summit 2015 Logo Contest again.

Look forward to seeing you in Seoul this October!
